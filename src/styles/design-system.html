How do you source footage from disaster zones? ▾
We partner with news agencies, survivor families, and documentary archives worldwide. All footage is licensed, credited, and used with express permission from rights holders.
Are your documentaries suitable for younger viewers? ▾
Content varies by episode. Each video is clearly rated and includes content advisories. We recommend parental guidance for viewers under 16.

The best way to support VillageFive is to subscribe, watch the full videos, and share them. Membership tiers are also available for exclusive content.
